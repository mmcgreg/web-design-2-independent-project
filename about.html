Who We Are

The Brew Table Restaurant and Bar has been a mainstay of Bells Corners for over 10 years. Starting out as a D'Arcy McGee's Irish pub, the transition to an independent restaurant and thus a rebrand allowed for The Brew Table to meet the needs of its patrons like never before. Striving for excellence in both service and food, The Brew Table brings the best of the industry to your table, serving up delicious dishes and world class service surrounded by a modern and inviting atmosphere.

The new location at 308 Moodie Drive has be outfitted with industry leading kitchen technology to help create new and exciting gastro pub inspired dishes.

With the new building being built from the ground up to specifications designed to best serve the needs of our clientele. The bar features 30 on demand draught taps with beers from all over Ontario as well as a wide selections of imports as well. Rotating seasonal taps will change as new and exciting draughts are brought to our attention from some of our awesome local brewery partners!
