Michaela Castillo
Michaela has been with the restaurant for 4 years. She is currently studying to become an electrical engineer and is a part of The Brew Crew during the evenings and weekends. Favourite dish: Short Rib Parpardelle

Devin West
Devin is coming up on 6 years with The Brew Table. He is a FOH supervisor as well as night bartender. In his free time he enjoys coming up with new and exciting cocktails and taking his border collie, Sam, for runs in the forest.

Nathaniel Barnett
Nate is the newest Brew Crew member. You'll find him on the floor in the evenings after he finishes up classes studying to become a graphic designer at Algonquin College. He's got a huge draught beer repertoire. Ask him about his favourite draught feature!

Kimmy Holmes
Kimmy is the Brew Crew's hostess with the mostest. She runs the show at the door, checking all our valued guest's vaccine passports. Always ready with a smile, Kimmy's grace and can-do attitude has been instrumental throughout the Covid-19 pandemic. Favourite dish: Naan Turkey Club with sweet potato fries

Elizabeth Decker
Liz is one of The Brew Table's FOH managers. She's been with the restaurant for over 10 years and is an integral part of the team. She is also our in-house social media manager, having received her degree in communications from U Ottawa in 2018. Next time your in, make sure to look around. If ABBA is playing, she'll be dancing!

Sam West
The unofficial/official Brew Table mascot. Sam is the friendly face you'll see on our social media accounts! Devin brings him around in the summer, when the patio is in full swing. You'll usually find him sniffing up any wayward fries that have dropped from busy servers plates. Favourite dish: Anything and everything
